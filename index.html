The Mythology of Centaurs

Centaurs are creatures from Greek mythology with the upper body of a human and the lower body of a horse. They were often depicted as wild and free spirits roaming the mountains and forests. Some legends portray them as wise teachers, while others describe them as fiercely untamed.
